Current projects

Asgeir R. Helgason and his collaborators are currently engaged in a number of projects in the fields of public health, social medicine and psychology.

Palliative end-of-life care

We are conducting a project with focus on male patients and methods for communication between patients, significant others and health care personnel. Project leader in Sweden is Ásgeir R. Helgason and Dr. Bragi Skúlason heads the project in Iceland. The project, is called “Meeting men and their significant others in palliative cancer care”. Financed by the Kamprad Foundation.

Tobacco and alcohol prevention

Primary prevention of health risk behaviors in adolescents, including tobacco and alcohol use. Secondary prevention mainly focusing on smoking cessation programs including the Swedish National Quitline (Sluta-Röka-Linjen) and smoking cessation activities in primary health care and dentistry.

Research on electronic screen use

We are researching electronic screen use and its effect on somatic and neurological symptoms in children.
